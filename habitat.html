Habitat For Humanity Huronia

I have had the opportunity to volunteer my graphic design services for Habitat For Humanity Huronia. While researching HFH’s brand regulations, I noted the current colour hex codes, typefaces, logo variations with all of their proper uses. Exploring with layout design within the strict guidelines already set in place with my supervisors’ inputs, I get to really focus and develop the working design at hand. An important note to follow here was to reduce the amount of ink to limit printing cost.
